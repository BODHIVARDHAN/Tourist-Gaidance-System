 Main artical:-Navegaon National Park
 Stretching his foot below the embankments, Hanuman epitomizing strength sits in his temple on the banks of Navegaon Lake, life giving water mass at Navegaon National Park in Gondia. Surrounded by seven peaks (thus the name Sat Bahini), considered to be seven sisters, the Navegaon Lake was supposed to have been constructed by Kolu Patel Kohli at the beginning of 18th century. Standing beside this Lake, one can get a magnificent glimpse of Wild Life at Navegaon National Park in Gondia, with its rare avian species flying above some richly hued floral varieties amidst which resides an amiable fauna family.
Navegaon National Park in Gondia consists of Deer Park, An Aviary, Three Beautiful Gardens and Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary. Covering an area of 135 square kilometers, Watch Towers are located at suitable points to enable to proper view of wild life. From the raiding Pindaris, a tribe of robbers, the local villagers used to obtain respite by escaping into this Navegaon National Park in Gondia.
Dr Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary located on the foothills of this region is a wonder for the tourists; characterized by the quacking ducks which migrate during winter over here, the white hue of their soft feathers swathes this region. The flora consists of Teak, Kawat, Mahua, Bhel Jamun, Haldu, and Bhor, while the fauna consists of Tigers, Sambars, Nilgais, Chitals, Wild boars, Panthers, Bisons, Sloth Bears, and Wild Dogs. They are natural pageantry jointly residing in this wild habitat at Navagon.
The Tiger Reserves in India is one of the projects undertaken by the wildlife lovers to protect the Indian tigers. The Tiger Reserves of India from the 70s have undertaken the job to protect the tigers and for this the Project Tiger and Wildlife Protection Act has been put into use. In order to save this predator from the harmful poachers the Tiger Reserves in India have taken up measures. The Project Tiger was launched in the year 1972 for the conservation of tigers. There are a number of tiger reserves enlisted under this project.
Tigers are the predators who maintain the balance of the population of other species. Carnivores are quite important to our eco system to maintain this balance and tigers therefore should be conserved. So there are a number of tiger reserves in India which are working on the conservation of tigers. The Tiger Reserves along with the conservation of tigers also have a rich variety of flora and fauna. Tourists love to visit the tiger reserves in India to get a glimpse of these royal creatures.
